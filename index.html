Life is not always easy. And sometimes it`s always hard. But there`s always a solution

Midwest Psychology Group is a growing collective of highly trained and compassionate professionals who care deeply about reducing human suffering and increasing human flourishing. We embrace people of all abilities, sizes, races, ethnicities, citizenships, religions, cultural, and sexual orientations, and respect individuals` preferred gender identities. We draw from the latest evidence-based strategies and work from various theoretical orientations. We offer in-person therapy for clients living in and near Bloomington, Indiana, and online therapy for clients across the state of Indiana. We also have a limited number of spaces available for clients located outside of Indiana through the Psychology Interjurisdictional Compact
